Why FenTrack

Scalable

FenTrack was developed to be readily scalable with all your company needs without it slowing down. You need not be bound by restrictive management and logistics platforms any longer.

Simple

Based on the features you require we tailor FenTrack to suit your business. It isn't an off-the-shelf package where you must adapt to the various (otherwise useless) features built for other people. We can also tailor the system so the reports you need are available at the touch of a button.

Robust

We install FenTrack onto a dedicated server specifically for your performance requirements. With this, your platform will continue to be quick, stable and secure for the future.

Affordable 

Using low-cost methods of tracking throughout manufacture and delivery, FenTrack keeps operational costs low without the need for constant mobile data. Handheld terminal tracking can be achieved using GPS coordinates through SMS.

Keep Your Hardware

FenTrack is built to work with your current hardware. We understand it is costly to upgrade hardware to meet the requirements of a new system; this is why FenTrack was built differently.

Owned License

Continuous monthly expenditure on a system which operates as a service (SaaS) is not economical for business. FenTrack can be purchased as a completely owned license software which becomes your asset and significantly reduces your monthly operating costs.

Customer Delivery Tracking

Customer Communication

Ingenious Efficiency

Simple Technology

Printable barcodes identify parts required for production/assembly. Coupled with the FenTrack android app on handheld terminals, the barcodes are quickly scanned to ensure the total order requirement is captured. This means no pieces are wrongly processed or missed for the order at hand. This reduces waste and increases efficiency through timely, organised production.
